Weightless Wonder -- Reduced-Gravity Flight
Math and Science @ Work presents a free-response-styled question for advanced high school physics. Students will learn about the parabolic flights of NASA's C-9 jet, the Weightless Wonder, as they use equations of motion to derive a solution to a real-life problem.
